Brief Summary

Prime Meals is a San Jose local food prep service where customers can sign up for a meal plan for pickup thats healthy, fresh, and fits with their goals.

Technologies

Shopify

An e-commerce platform for online stores and retail point-of-sale systems.

Overview




I designed the layout of the site, created the overall branding and logo, uploaded the meal plans, and setup a subscription service using a Shopify app called Subscriptions by ReCharge.

I was hired onto this project to create an ecommerce site for this business that would allow customers to place orders and subcribe to meal plans. Therefore, the website would have to be able to take payment, print out orders, and implement repeated sales based on saved subcriptions. Therefore, in stead of building this application from the ground up, I realized that the fasted way to get this up and running would be to use Shopify.
